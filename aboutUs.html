About Carbon Ledger 🌱

Carbon Ledger helps individuals understand and track their carbon footprint. By measuring everyday activities such as travel, electricity use, and lifestyle choices, our platform provides insight into how these actions impact the environment.

🌍 Our Mission

To raise awareness about carbon emissions and help people make sustainable decisions that protect our planet.

📊 What We Do

We provide tools that calculate carbon emissions from daily activities so users can track and reduce their environmental impact.

🌱 Our Vision

A future where everyone actively monitors their carbon footprint and contributes toward a greener planet.
